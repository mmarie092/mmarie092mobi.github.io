This website started out as a web authoring project for a class and grew into a--actually, it is still just a web authoring project. But an educational one!


here at mmarie092.github.io, we strive to educate potential cat owners who struggle with allergies. We believe that just because nature can be unforgiving, you don't always have to be restricted to owning a dog, fish, or lizard when your heart knows it wants a feline friend.


The author of this website is a dedicated cat-mom who is married to an unfortunate man that is allergic to most living things on this planet. It is through trial and error (and a little spouse experimentation) that she strives to make both his life and the life of her cats the best it can be!
