How do I get a cat that produces less Fel D 1?:

First, I recommend checking out the Cat Breeds page to look at some breeds that are more suitable for people with allergies. There are more breeds out there, so check out a few more sites and do some research. Then, find a breeder for the specific cat you want.

I know what breed of cat I want! How do I find a breeder?:

Great! Now that you know what kind of cat you are looking for, now you need to find a breeder. You can use the Find a Breeder tool on TICA's(The International Cat Association) website to help locate a registered breeder closest to you.

REMEMBER! Be wary of scammers and kitten mills that are only out to make a quick buck without regard to their cats' wellbeing. Research, read reviews, and make sure the breeder you have selected is reputable.

Are Hypoallergenic Cats Expensive?:

Buying any animal from a reputable breeder is expensive. Cats are no exception. Most hypoallergenic cat breeds will run between $1,400 and $2,000 per kitten.

Ya know, I'd rather adopt than find a breeder. What about allergy shots?:

Allergy shots, or allergen immunotherapy, can help reduce symptoms associated with cat allergies. You can get a consultation from a doctor and see what your options are. Just remember that this is long-term treatment. You may have to spend a few years and a few thousand dollars to keep up with shots and get the most out of the treatment.
